About JMARA

Proudly Indigenous owned and operated — delivering quality equipment hire and material supply.

JMARA is a proudly Indigenous owned and operated business committed to delivering high‑quality equipment hire and material supply services across Victoria and the broader construction and infrastructure sectors. Founded on the values of respect, reliability, and cultural integrity, we create meaningful partnerships while supporting sustainable development and Indigenous economic empowerment.

With deep‑rooted connections to Country and a strong understanding of local landscapes and industries, we bring a unique perspective to every project. Our team combines decades of industry experience with traditional knowledge, offering a reliable, safety‑focused, and community‑driven approach to service.

We supply a broad range of materials and offer well‑maintained equipment for hire, supporting projects in mining, civil construction, infrastructure and beyond. Whether you’re seeking timely delivery, customised solutions, or long‑term collaboration, JMARA stands ready to meet your needs with professionalism and pride.

As we grow, we remain committed to creating opportunities for Indigenous employment, training, and partnerships – contributing to a stronger, more inclusive future for all.

Our Mission

To deliver trusted equipment and materials solutions while creating lasting benefit for our people, clients and communities.

Our Vision

A thriving Indigenous business leading with culture, capability and care.
