About JMARA

Indigenous-owned and operated. Respect, reliability and cultural integrity at the core.

JMARA Plant Hire and Supply Solutions Pty Ltd is an Indigenous owned and operated business based in Wildwood, Melbourne, Victoria. Executive Director Alice Lorraine Forsyth is a proud Gunditjmara descendent and Indigenous representative for the Gunditj Mirring Traditional Owners Corporation.

Founded on the values of respect, reliability, and cultural integrity, we aim to create meaningful partnerships while supporting sustainable development and economic empowerment within Indigenous communities.

We combine decades of industry experience with traditional knowledge, bringing a reliable, safety‑focused and community-driven approach to every project. As we grow, we remain committed to Indigenous employment, training and partnerships — contributing to a stronger, more inclusive future.
